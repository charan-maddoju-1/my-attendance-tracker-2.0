Welcome to the Application !

Hi !
Here is you attendance tracker. By using this application you can make record of you attendance count and track the progress in reaching your attendance goal.

How it works ?

  • Set you attendance goal
  • Add subjects
  • On attending classes, increase both attended and total classes count by one for that specific subject
  • On missing a class,increase only the total classes count for that specific subject

On every action you perform this application will reflect the percentage of attendance you had in each subject individually and it indicates you in which subject you have lack of attendance.
Also it shows the overall attendance in percentage and indicates whether you have reached your attendance goal.
